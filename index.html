⛳ User Guide

1. Setup & Players

• Search or Add Course Name, Par, and Stroke Index (SI). Points are calculated automatically based on these.

• Add players and handicaps. Edit them anytime in the 'Setup' tab.

2. Scoring a Round

• Enter Gross Strokes for each hole in the 'Enter Scores' tab.

• Awards: When entering a score, select Yes or No to award trophies for 'Longest Drive' or 'Nearest Pin'.

3. Tie-Breakers (Countback)

Ties are settled by: Back 9 > Last 6 > Last 3 > 18th Hole.

4. Automatic Handicaps

• Winner: Handicap cut by 2.0.

• Last Place: Handicap increased by 1.0.

5. Matchplay Knockouts

• Click names to advance. Record final scores (e.g., 4&3) in the box before saving.
